Grand Rapids

I live in Grand Rapids, Michigan. I love my city. There are always things to do here, especially for families.

Here are some things to explore in Grand Rapids

Rosa Parks Circle

You can visit Rosa Parks Circle for many acivities. You can watch a concert, enjoy swing dancing, join an event, join a rally, or just hang out and grab lunch at a surrounding venue. During the winter months you can grab your ice skates and go ice skating! There are endless opportunities for fun at Rosa Parks Circle!

Grand Rapids Art Museum

Looking for a day of admiring art? How about creating art for yourself? You can bring the whole family to check out their Drop-in studio! Check out what events and exhibitions the Grand Rapids Art Museum has to offer!


Fredrick Meijer Gardens

Enjoy being emersed in the horticultural display gardens and sculpture park. Check out the many events and activities for kids and families. Definately check out their butterfly exhibit in March, enjoy story telling time, use your senses in their sense garden and so much more!
Childrens Museum

A place for young children to stay safe and have fun! Some much to check out here including their play marketplace and many interactive stations! A great place where young kids can play!
